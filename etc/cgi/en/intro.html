Registration 1/3

We provide Usenet to interested netizens, free of charge. You can register an account with this automated form in a couple of minutes. You will need to:

  • Specify a working email address.
    Registration is completed by replying to a mail sent by us.
  • Solve a simple picture puzzle (Captcha).
    If you are unable to do so, send your request as a plain e-mail to news@albasani.net. It will processed by a human, please be patient.
  • Accept our terms of use.
